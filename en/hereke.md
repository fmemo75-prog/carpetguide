## The Six Stages of Perfection

To understand why Hereke is the "Shah of Carpets," we must look at its painstaking 6-step journey:

### Technical Excellence
- **Origin:** Hereke, Izmit (South of Istanbul).
- **Material:** 100% Pure Silk on Silk or fine wool.
- **Knot Type:** Ghiordes (Turkish) Double Knot.
- **Identity:** The choice of Emperors, Tsars, and Kings for nearly two centuries.
