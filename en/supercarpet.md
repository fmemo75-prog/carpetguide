# 🏺 The Art of Carpets: Masterpieces of Anatolia

Every knotted rug is a physical manifestation of time and labor. To understand our collection, you must understand the three "Masterpieces of Anatolia."

---

## ❄️ 1. The Pazyryk Carpet: The "Birth Certificate" of Rug Art
The world's oldest knotted carpet (5th Century BC) is the ultimate proof of our heritage.

![Pazyryk Map](./Pazyryk_numbered.png)

*   **The Discovery:** Found in Siberia, preserved in ice for 2500 years.
*   **The Technical Proof:** It features the **Ghiordes (Turkish) Double Knot**.
*   **The Motifs:** 28 horsemen and grazing stags—symbols of power and life.

---

## 👑 2. Regional Masterpieces: The Big Three

### A. HEREKE: The Sultan’s Choice 👑
*   **Historical Prestige:** Established in 1843. Woven exclusively for Ottoman Palaces.
*   **The Material:** Usually **Silk on Silk**.
*   **Technical Marvel:** Famous for unrivaled knot density (1 million+ knots).

### B. UŞAK (OUSHAK): The Star of Europe ⭐
*   **Renaissance Icon:** Appeared in paintings by **Holbein and Vermeer**.
*   **Design:** Famous for grand scale **Star and Medallion** patterns.
*   **Trend:** Interior designers' favorite for soft pastel colors.

### C. BERGAMA: The Nomadic Soul ⛰️
*   **Ancient Heritage:** Shamanistic and tribal motifs from ancient Pergamon.
*   **The Palette:** 100% natural **Root Dyes** (Madder red, Indigo blue).
*   **Feel:** 100% wool, thick and authentic.
