<a name="types"></a>
## 🏆 Masterpieces of Anatolia (Famous Rug Types)

1.  **Hereke: The Palace Silk 👑** - Originally made only for Ottoman Sultans. Known for extremely high knot density and intricate silk patterns.
2.  **Uşak (Oushak): The Star of Europe ⭐** - The favorite of Renaissance painters. Known for soft pastel colors and grand medallion designs.
3.  **Bergama: The Nomadic Spirit ⛰️** - Reflects the bold, geometric designs of nomadic tribes using 100% natural wool and root dyes.

<a name="quality"></a>
## 🔍 Buyer's Guide: How to Spot Quality
1.  **Check the Back:** A handmade rug's pattern is almost as clear on the back as on the front.
2.  **The "Abrash" Effect:** Slight color variations in the same shade are a sign of natural dyes. This is a mark of value!
3.  **Knot Density:** Flip the rug and look at the knots per square inch. More knots = More labor.
