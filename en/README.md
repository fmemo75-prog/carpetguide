# 🧶 Global Carpet & Kilim Guide (English)

Welcome to the open-source encyclopedia of weaving arts. This guide is designed to help you understand the history, craftsmanship, and value of handmade rugs.

## 🏺 The Timeless Legacy: From Pazyryk to Your Home

Buying a handmade rug is not just an interior design choice; it is an acquisition of a 2,500-year-old human heritage.

### 🔍 Deep Dive: Visual Map of the Pazyryk Carpet

![The Pazyryk Carpet Map](./Pazyryk_numbered.png)

| No | Visual Zone | Element | Symbolism & Historical Fact |
| :-- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | **The Center** | **24 Rosettes** | Each square represents a "lotus" or solar symbol. It signifies the cycle of eternal life and the sun. |
| **2** | **Inner Border** | **Procession of Stags** | These spotted fallow deer (elks) represent the spiritual world and grace in ancient nomadic culture. |
| **3** | **Outer Border** | **The Horsemen** | 28 riders and grooms are depicted, reflecting the high social status and military power of the Scythian tribes. |

## 🏛️ Why This History Matters Today
The **Pazyryk Carpet** (found in Siberia, dated 5th Century BC) proves that a well-made rug is an **immortal heirloom**. Even after 2500 years, its sophisticated "Ghiordes" double knots and vibrant colors are still visible.

When you buy a handmade rug today, you are using the same techniques used by ancient kings.

Choosing a handmade rug is more than buying furniture; it is an investment in a durable asset.

| Feature | 🧶 Handmade Rug | 🤖 Machine-Made Rug |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Durability** | **50 - 100+ Years.** Gets better with age. | **5 - 10 Years.** Deteriorates quickly. |
| **Investment** | Becomes an **"Antique"**. Value increases. | Depreciates to **Zero** value instantly. |

## 🏆 Masterpieces of Anatolia (Famous Rug Types)

### 1. Hereke: The Palace Silk 👑
Originally made only for Ottoman Sultans. Known for extremely high knot density and intricate silk patterns.

### 2. Uşak (Oushak): The Star of Europe ⭐
The favorite of Renaissance painters. Known for soft pastel colors and grand medallion designs.

### 3. Bergama: The Nomadic Spirit ⛰️
Reflects the bold, geometric designs of nomadic tribes using 100% natural wool and root dyes.
