### ❄️ Frozen in Time: Why This Rug Changed History
The **Pazyryk Carpet** is the world’s oldest known knotted rug, dating back to the **5th Century BC**. Trapped in a frozen tomb for **2,500 years**, the rug was preserved in solid ice. This miracle of nature allows us to see the **Ghiordes (Turkish) Double Knot** technique exactly as it was perfected millennia ago.
