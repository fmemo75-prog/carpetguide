Slow Motion Analysis
The Ghiordes (Turkish) Double Knot
▶
Step by Step
01
Place the yarn
Fold a cut length of yarn over two adjacent warp threads.
02
Wrap left
Loop the left end around the left warp thread, pulling it forward and down.
03
Wrap right
Loop the right end around the right warp thread — forming the Ω shape.
04
Pull & secure
Pull both ends down firmly. Double knot complete — 2 warp threads locked.
05
Single knot variation
Same technique on one warp thread — used in finer, denser pile work.
A master weaver ties up to 10,000 knots per day.
