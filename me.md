### 📜 Professional Profile & Background

**Fatih Mehmet Canıtez** is a dedicated professional educator and rug expert with over **25 years of experience** in French language teaching. He holds a **Master’s degree from Hacettepe University**, one of Turkey's most prestigious academic institutions, providing a rigorous intellectual foundation to his work.

Based in **Eskişehir since 2006**, Fatih spends his summers in the heart of the weaving world: **Göreme, Cappadocia**. Here, he focuses deeply on traditional Turkish carpets and handicrafts, merging his academic discipline with his lifelong passion for Anatolian heritage.

As a true **polyglot**, his expertise extends beyond French. He is highly proficient in **Italian and Spanish**, offering instruction and guidance in both. Furthermore, he has spent many years teaching **Turkish to foreigners**, helping international students connect with Turkish culture through language.

Beyond the classroom and the loom, Fatih shares his linguistic knowledge globally via **YouTube**, where he creates structured educational content for French, Italian, and Turkish learners.

By bridging his **linguistic mastery** with **deep technical knowledge of rug arts**, he provides a unique, expert-level perspective for global collectors and travelers visiting Cappadocia. Whether through a language lesson or a rug consultation, Fatih serves as a cultural ambassador, preserving and explaining the "woven poems" of Anatolia to the world.
